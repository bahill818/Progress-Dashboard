Wolf & Wildlife Safari

A private guided morning in Lamar Valley — the Serengeti of North America. Professional spotting scopes, 6–8 hours, just the four of us and a wildlife biologist.
What we could see
Wolf packs · grizzly bears · bison with brand-new calves · elk · pronghorn · bald eagles · coyotes
🐺 Mom's bucket list
The Junction Butte and Lamar Canyon packs are the most reliably spotted in the park — and late May is prime wolf season
Book it
In Our Nature or Lamar Valley Touring · reserve 2–3 months ahead for a private group of 4

Kayaks Beneath
the Tetons

Guided paddle on glass-calm Jackson Lake with Mount Moran — all 12,605 feet of it — reflected around the boats. Afternoon boat shuttle across Jenny Lake to Hidden Falls and Inspiration Point.
The morning
Private kayak tour, group of 4 · all gear included · eagles, osprey, and river otters along 15+ named islands
The afternoon
Jenny Lake boat ➜ short climb to a 100-ft waterfall ➜ Inspiration Point's canyon views
Why 8:30 AM
Morning water is flat calm — that's when the Tetons turn into a mirror
